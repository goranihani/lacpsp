# Legal AI Tool Routing

Use tools by job, not by habit.

| Tool Type | Best Use | Do Not Use For |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ChatGPT/basic GPT | second reader, issue compression, counterarguments, AI-usage report wording | final legal authority, exact citations from memory |
| LBOX or legal search AI | Korean cases/statutes and similar-situation search | unquestioned final answer |
| Super Lawyer or similar legal AI | issue spotting, practical measures, opposing arguments | confirmed statute/case status without official check |
| AiLex or structured legal AI | fact organization, outline, missing question check | replacing human legal judgment |
| Official DB/search | final verification of statutes, cases, regulator pages | broad brainstorming |

## Track A / Track B

Track A owns:

- direct conclusions
- answer structure
- application to facts
- counterarguments and rebuttals
- final text

Track B owns:

- AI prompts
- official source checks
- `CONFIRM/MODIFY/HOLD/DELETE/GENERAL` coding
- AI-usage ledger
- submission format, anonymity, and timing

## Candidate Hand-Off Format

```text
Q-ID:
Candidate:
Source to verify:
Status: CONFIRM / MODIFY / HOLD / DELETE / GENERAL
Answer treatment:
AI-usage report material:
```
