# Legal AI Candidate Search Prompt

Use for LBOX, Super Lawyer, AiLex, or an allowed legal AI. Ask for candidates, not a final memo.

```text
For the issue below, provide only legal authority candidates and opposing arguments.
Do not write a final answer.
Do not add facts not in the problem.
If you give a statute, case, regulator document, deadline, or article number, mark it as VERIFY until checked against an official source.

Return:
Candidate | Why relevant | Official source to check | Possible counterargument | Risk if unverified

Issue:
Facts:
```
