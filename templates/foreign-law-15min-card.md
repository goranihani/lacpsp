# Foreign-Law 15-Minute Card

Use if the problem shows foreign users, overseas transfers, foreign regulators, international payments, AI risk, or cross-border data.

## 00-02 Minutes

| Signal | Jurisdiction | Keep? |
| --- | --- | --- |
| EU market/user/regulator | EU/Germany | yes/no |
| US financial data/security | US | yes/no |
| Japan user/data/payment | Japan | yes/no |
| China user/data/export | China | yes/no |
| AI/AML/payment standard | International | yes/no |

## 02-10 Minutes

| Source ID | Proposition | Official source path | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
|  |  |  | CONFIRM/MODIFY/HOLD/DELETE/GENERAL |

## 10-15 Minutes

| Status | Answer wording |
| --- | --- |
| CONFIRM | use narrowly |
| MODIFY | correct and qualify |
| HOLD | mention as risk or omit |
| DELETE | remove |
| GENERAL | use as non-binding practical standard |

## Closing Sentence

```text
Foreign-law points are limited to verified source IDs. Unverified deadlines, sanctions, licensing duties, and AI classifications are HOLD or removed.
```
